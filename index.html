The new era of arcade controllers.

The first thing that comes with playing fighting games is your "weapon" of choice. In this case, it's your controller. There are many different kinds of controllers availableat varying price points. Choose one that you feel you would like to learn with.

Everyone has their own method of learning. Be it hands-on, visual, or even audio, your choice in controller should help you in the way you're best at learning. There's no advantage to one or the other. It's all preference.

    What controller should you use?
  • If you want to preserve the legacy or experience the novelty of the arcade era. A classic arcade stick is the choice for you.
  • Convenience, portability, reliability, you can't go wrong with a traditional gamepad.
  • If you want the benefits of an arcade stick but with more, and you don't mind the learning curve, go for a leverless.
